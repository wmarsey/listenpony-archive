Listenpony is a concert series, commissioning body and record label run by three composers, William Marsey, Josephine Stephenson, and Freya Waley-Cohen. We host incredible musicians and curate concerts of new and old classical music alongside other genres including folk, jazz, and pop. We write and commissioning new works for each event, and programme the nights into multiple short sets, to create an inimitably relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.
