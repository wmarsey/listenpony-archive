Listenpony #17: Tabea Debus + Apartment House + Addelam

19:00 Monday 28 January 2019
Crypt on the Green
Clerkenwell Close, London EC1R 0EA

Live at Listenpony: Tabea Debus
4 Oct 2019

Recording available digitally

Listenpony returns to Crypt on the Green with recorder soloist Tabea Debus, a string trio from experimental chamber group Apartment House and a new project from cellist Colin Alexander and composer Beni Giles, Addelam.

Alongside music by Bach, Telemann and Jean Françaix, you will hear new works by Freya Waley-Cohen, Alex Paxton, Josephine Stephenson, William Marsey and Liam Mattison as well as extracts from Addelam's upcoming first record.
