Listenpony #21: James Larter + Explore Ensemble + Sophie Jamieson

19:30 Thursday 3 March 2022
Crypt on the Green
Clerkenwell Close, London EC1R 0EA

For Listenpony #21 we hosted a trio from the incredible Explore Ensemble, percussionist and multi-instrumentalist James Larter, and singer-songwriter Sophie Jamieson.

Explore Ensemble brought us a programme of new and old music, including new commissions from William Marsey and Isabella Gellis. James Larter will be performing Xenakis, Michael Gordon, and a new commission from Seán Clancy.

The evening ended with a set from the "hauntingly evocative" singer-songwriter Sophie Jamieson.
